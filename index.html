Interesting Facts About the Panda

  1. Pandas or giant pandas are native to South Central China.
  2. Pandas are no longer endangered species but they are still vulnerable.
  3. These great creatures spend a lot of the day eating.

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excelllent hearing abilities.
